frequently asked questions

What subjects are offered on the platform?

The platform provides a wide range of subjects including computer science, languages and coding. It caters to school, college, and exam-specific courses like GCSE, IGCSE, AS, and A Level.

How can I book a session with the tutor?

You can fill the website's form and we will shortly reply you after that and choose an available time slot. You can also book a session with the tutor.

How much are the fees?

Fees vary depending on the subject, tutor experience, and level of the course. You can also bargain the price with a tutor. Some courses also offer discounted packages for multiple sessions.

Does the platform offer online and offline classes?

Currently, all classes are conducted online through video sessions, allowing students to learn from anywhere. In the future, the platform plans to introduce offline, in-person classes at selected locations.

What equipment do I need for online classes?

You only need a device such as a laptop, tablet, or smartphone with a stable internet connection. A headset and webcam are recommended for the best learning experience, but most classes can be joined with basic audio and video.

Is there a trial class available?

Yes, a trial or demo class is available to allow students to assess teaching style and course content before committing to a full session.
